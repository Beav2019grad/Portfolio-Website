About Me

Forest Friends is a group project for a graduate level wood science class at Oregon State University. The goal was to have something made out of 90% renewable materials, and it has to be proven to work. Our instructor Seri Robinson wanted no toxic finishes, paints, or inks on this project as well. This toy is made out of dark cherry wood, which does not give out harmful extractives. Dark cherry wood also has a natural darker color, which avoids the need for stains or overcoats. The drum ens was made out of scrap rawhide. Last I woodburned my designs on all components, ehich avoids the use of processed inks.
